What is NDNU Study Groups?

NDNU Study Groups is a website that has been designed and developed with everything you need to get access to your StudyGroup schedule. Whether you need a particular time, specific place or unique class topic to fit your needs, StudyGroup has the tools and resources you need to setup the perfect meetup.

Why choose NDNU Study Groups?

Trying to find like minded individuals and organize a meetup with others should not be a difficult and frustrating job in itself. Our in-house design team came up with the perfect solution and created this awesome Studyroup Tool. The simple idea of connecting individuals with similar needs is a result of our teams extensive task of research, development and testing. We are working to create a reliable and useful website that has the tools you need to find the help you need.

Our History

Our team started to develop this website with the same goals in mind. We realized it was difficult to find an existing tool that was usuable and simple to use. This website is created for students, by students.
